# M813 TMA 01 Preparation – Notes & Checklist

---

## 1. Key Tasks for TMA 01 Preparation

- **Read TMA 01 thoroughly** – annotate requirements, deliverables, and deadlines.
- Understand all artifacts required (diagrams, prose, stakeholder/process explanations).

---

## 2. Choosing Organisation & Problem Domain

- **Select an organisation:**  
  - Ideally your workplace, but any familiar or hypothetical organisation is allowed.
  - Example domains: retail/catalogue/order, document management, rental/loan systems, booking systems, club/library management.
- **Identify a business problem:**  
  - Must be used by people, process and track data, and support real business processes.
  - Examples: submission/review, member management, booking flows.
- **Start small, expand if needed.**

---

---

## 4. Ethics & Legal Preparation

- [x] No real personal data (make data anonymous/fake if needed).
- [x] No vulnerable stakeholders (children, etc.).
- [x] Use roles/titles/pseudonyms instead of real names.
- [x] Get informed consent for any stakeholder involvement, even if informal.
- [x] Note risks & mitigations (health, confidentiality, data protection).

---

## 5. Forum Post Preparation (Synopsis)

- Prepare a 300-word synopsis for the tutor forum.
- Include these:
  - Organisation overview
  - Business problem/scenario
  - Key processes
  - Stakeholders/roles
  - Justification for scope and fit to M813
  - Note on ethics/anonymisation
- Label with your name + PI number.
- Wait for tutor approval before finalising answer.

## 8. What to Avoid

- [x] Too simple (“todo list”, generic calculator, or without real user/process data context)
- [x] Anything with confidential/personal data, or involving vulnerable people
- [x] Any scenario you cannot realistically prototype, model or explain
